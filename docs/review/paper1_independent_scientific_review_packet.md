# Paper 1 independent scientific review packet

Status: awaiting external reviewer assignment

## Requested expertise

At least one independent researcher with expertise in terrestrial gravimetry,
ocean loading or physical geodesy. The reviewer should not be an author and
should disclose collaboration or financial conflicts.

## Review scope

This is a scientific review, not a code-style review. Please assess:

- reference frame, sign and Green-function conventions;
- separation of direct, elastic/potential, height and gradient observables;
- interpretation of the Helgoland published comparison;
- spectral-energy coverage definition and threshold sensitivity;
- whether representative process scenarios are described without population
  or universal-detectability claims;
- instrument-curve provenance and non-extrapolation;
- novelty and adequacy of the related-work positioning.
