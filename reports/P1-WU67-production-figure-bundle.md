# P1-WU67 Paper 1 production figure bundle

Date: 2026-07-12

Source experiment: `P1-E006-evidence-bounded-atlas`

Four deterministic production figures and PNG review previews were generated:

1. process characteristic frequencies versus admitted instrument-curve support;
2. evidence-bounded direct-gravity amplitude envelopes versus spherical surface
   standoff;
3. the process--instrument signal-energy coverage matrix; and
4. internal-wave, tsunami and Storegga sensitivity envelopes.

The renderer was executed twice with identical asset checksums. Visual review of
all four PNG previews passed for label visibility, log-axis readability, colour
separation and consistency with the machine-readable metrics. The frequency
panel explicitly shows the admitted `1e-3 Hz` lower curve edge; the matrix states
that no SNR classification is allowed below 90% energy coverage. Envelope
shading is labelled as sensitivity rather than probability.

These are production primary-branch figures. Structural variants listed in
`P1-E006` remain necessary before deciding whether the amplitude/sensitivity
panels are final manuscript figures. The coverage panel and coverage matrix do
not depend on IGETS, Haikou observations, or PEGS simulation.
